All eportfolios should have an About Me page. Without identification, the content and reflection can't be associated with you. Let's face it, writing a brief bio is a pain. What do you include? How do you keep it from revealing too much about you? Where do you start?

Here is one option for creating an about me page:

Title your page, "Five Facts About Me" and bullet point or number five facts that highlight your strengths, character, interests, and leadership abilities. Vary the five facts to cover different areas of your life. You don't have to reveal too much about yourself; keep it generic and creative.
