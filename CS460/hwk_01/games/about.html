about Old-School Gaming.....

nowdays when people talk about "gaming", or being "gamers", they're almost always talking about video or computer games.

not us! we are old-school gamers..... we like board games and card games... puzzle games and trivia games... word games and fun, laugh-out-loud party games...

draw something, sculpt something, act something out, fill-in-the-blank, spell something backwards, match words to categories or make patterns and collections.

family games nights are some of my favorite memories from growing up, and right up to the present day -- still making memories over gaming. and nothing is more fun to share laughs and make memories with friends, than playing a game!

they're a really good excuse for a pizza-night or a snack-day too..... if only you could come to one of our two-day New-Year's-Eve\New-Year's-Day two-day parties some year!

just remember a few simple guidelines:
  1. gaming is supposed to be *fun*!
    be competitive, but most of all, have fun!
  2. be nice! don't be a jerk when you're winning *or* losing!
  3. if you get into playing the same game over & over just because it's fun... you're missing out on all kinds of other fun from games you haven't tried yet, or haven't played in a while!
